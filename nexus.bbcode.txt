[size=4][color=#00ffff]OVERVIEW[/color][/size]
Unlike other left-hand ring mods, this plugin does not rely on a hand-made set of duplicate left-hand ring records. Instead, it builds the left-hand item from the actual ring in your inventory.

That means vanilla rings, DLC rings, Creation Club rings, mod-added rings, retextured rings, mesh-replaced rings, and custom enchanted rings can all work without needing dedicated patches, as long as they behave like normal Skyrim rings.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]BALANCE[/color][/size]
By default, left-hand ring enchantments use full strength.

An optional setting can reduce the left-hand ring’s enchantment strength while you are also wearing an enchanted right-hand ring. This is useful if you want the extra ring slot without doubling your enchanted-ring power.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]BOND OF MATRIMONY[/color][/size]
The Bond of Matrimony can use its own left-hand slot. Fully configurable. If enabled, the ring will use the left-hand ring finger.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]CONTROLS[/color][/size]
[list]
[*]Left-click a ring to toggle right hand equipment state.[/*]
[*]Right-click a ring to toggle left hand equipment state.[/*]
[/list]

If you only have one copy of a ring, selecting it for the left hand will move it off the right hand. If you have two matching copies, you can wear one on each hand.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]CONFIGURATION[/color][/size]
Edit the INI:

[quote]MCM\Config\LeftHandRingsSKSE\settings.ini[/quote]

Or use the MCM addon for in-game customization of the same settings:

[list]
[*]Left ring slot. Default: slot 58.[/*]
[*]Separate Bond of Matrimony slot. Default: enabled.[/*]
[*]Bond of Matrimony slot. Default: slot 59.[/*]
[*]Left ring enchantment strength while an enchanted right-hand ring is worn. Default: 100%.[/*]
[/list]

The slot menus include the standard commonly used mod body slots 44-61. If another mod uses the same slot, choose a different one.
